Welcome to Victoria Adler’s website!

Victoria Adler is an actor from Enköping, Sweden.

She started pursuing acting at age 14 when she had her first role on stage as Juliet in Shakespeare’s “Romeo and Juliet”.
She began her acting studies at Stockholm Art School (2010-2013). Alongside her studies she did her first on screen jobs.
She was in 2 seasons of the well received tv-show “Partaj” as a dancer and actor.
Her first on screen role was as Tiffany in JG CJ Productions “Bara Vara Jag” (2011).
She’s had smaller parts and worked as an extra in a number of films, tv-shows, theatre performances and music videos.

She also lived and worked in Boston, MA and took acting classes at JRP New England (2013-2014).

2017 she got accepted to The School of the Science of Acting in Archway, London where she’s studied both acting and directing.
She graduated 2021.

She is currently a part of the company Splats Entertainment/Shakespeare in a Suitcase, performing at schools around England.

She will also return as the First Witch and varoius new characters in Shadow Road Productions Macbeth in Edinburgh Fringe 6-12th August. See tickets below.

She is represented by British Talent Agency.

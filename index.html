Victoria Adler

actor


Victoria Adler is a Swedish actor from the small town of Enköping.

From her formative years, she immersed herself fully in narratives, whether films or books. She often daydreamed about the lives of the various characters. Little did she know this was the spark to her interest in acting.
Over time, films like "Lord of the Rings," "Troy," "The Last Samurai," and "Gladiator" not only captured her imagination but also ignited her fascination with swordsmanship. Additionally, BBC's rendition of "Robin Hood" came along and prompted her exploration of archery and historical reenactment.
At 15, Victoria discovered that acting was the gateway to participating in these captivating narratives. Her role as Juliet in Shakespeare's "Romeo and Juliet" marked her stage debut and propelled her forward without a moment's hesitation. Subsequently, this led her to audition to the acting program at Stockholm Art School (2010-2013).
Alongside her studies, Victoria entered the world of film. From roles in short films to numerous appearances as an Extra. This opened up her eyes to the profession and she never looked back. She was determined to become a part of it. Relocating to Boston, MA, after graduating, to work as an Au Pair while pursuing acting studies at JRP New England.

After a few years of gaining life experience, she started to audition to a number of drama schools. She secured a spot at The School of the Science of Acting 2017. This brought her to London and the city that has been her main base for the last 6 years. Graduating into a very different industry in 2020, Victoria has since then embodied several roles on stage and behind the camera. She frequently works with the company Splats Entertainment, where she’s performing Shakespeare plays and Pantos all around England.

Merits from 2023:
Shadow Road Productions “Macbeth” at St Mary’s, Barnes and at Edinburgh Fringe Festival
Teatro do Avessos “A Midsummer Night's Dream” at Museu Quinta das Cruzes, Madeira
Frisky Half Speed Productions “The Wanderer” at The Greenhouse Theatre, Canary Wharf

At present, Victoria’s embracing her childhood interests fully by specialising herself in stage and screen combat with BADC and BASSC. As well as archery with Archery Fit, where she has passed as a Pro Archer. Coming from a physical background, encompassing football, horse riding and dance, she thrives in physically demanding roles.
She will be touring in December and January performing across the UK as Robin Hood, in Splats Entertainment's Panto "Robin Hood"

Victoria welcomes professional collaboration and inquiries.
